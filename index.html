About Me

Greetings! I am Saul, an aspiring programmer currently in my first year of my degree in Games Programming at Staffordshire University.

I am an avid fan of Turn Based Strategy, MMOs and MOBAs, but my interests spill into all kinds of games. Alongside my interest in video games I also greatly enjoy tabletop games including D&D and board games.

When I'm not playing games, I can often be found programming them! I am currently involved in learning C++ and have been dabbling in C# using the Unity Engine.

Portfolio

My portfolio may be in its early days but it is ever expanding. Alongside my coursework I am undertaking personal projects as well as game jams as part of a larger team. Stay tuned for updates!

Astrorum Resurgemus - Week 1 Game Jam

The first week of our course saw us complete a game jam alongside fellow students. I met Aled, Jack and Haz which saw the formation of our team: Bring on The Wall!

We set out to create our game in just under a week. We wanted to create a relatively simple space shooter akin to classics such as Space Invaders and Galaga.

Our game was made using C# in the Unity Engine.

The sound effects were a particularly fun contribution to the game. I worked closely with the lads to ensure the final build ran correctly. This was the first game any of us had ever made. We were so chuffed when it was done, we couldn't stop playing it!

Astrorum Resurgemus - Star Rising

Taking direct inspiration from our week 1 game jam, I am making efforts to pursue Astrorum Resurgemus further! We had a lot more ideas than we had time to implement them, so I figured it would be a good place to start.

Whilst the retro-gameplay feel served us well for a first game, a more modern bullet-hell experience seemed an appropriate direction for the newly souped-up space shooter. The retro art style gets to stick around though, pixels all the way.

Star Rising is currently being made using C# in the Unity Engine, developing on the skills learned in the week 1 game jam.

Whilst I'm currently flying solo on Star Rising, I've reached out to the gang to continue the project someday. Perhaps when deadlines are out of the way!

Voidbeak

Voidbeak is a bit of an odd one. I have always enjoyed 2D Platformers and Metroidvania games. I was greatly inspired by Team Cherry's Hollow Knight during my earliest delve into development.

Whilst I would not consider myself an artist, I have taken up the digital brush to try and bring a bit of life to my 2D Platformer in-the-works so as to capture a fraction of what made Hollow Knight so striking.

I expect this project to be a strain on my creative muscles as much as it will test my programming resolve. As of now I have Voidbeak's character design, early sprite and basic animtions along with movement sorted. This one is pretty close to my heart, so it's definitely one to watch!
